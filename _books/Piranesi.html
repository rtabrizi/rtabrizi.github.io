--- layout: book title: Piranesi author: Susanna Clarke date: 2022-12-03 ---

I really enjoyed this book! The world building was pretty fascinating and Piranesi's aloofness was surprisingly calming in that he seems so happy in his tiny little world. Granted, we shouldn't strive for that mindset as that would be negligence of the world around us and our responsibilities. Still, it was nice to see how someone can be at peace with the world around them and even be extremely proud of it.
