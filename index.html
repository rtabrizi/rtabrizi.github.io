Ryan Tabrizi

email | scholar | linkedin | github

I'm a senior studying EECS at UC Berkeley, working on computer vision research with Angjoo Kanazawa and Alexei Efros. I previously worked with Kurt Keutzer on efficient ML frameworks. My interests are primarily in visual representations for world understanding, whether those be 3D, video, or embodied.

In the past I've worked as a research scientist at insitro (Core Imaging Team) and NASA JPL (Machine Learning and Instrument Autonomy Group).
